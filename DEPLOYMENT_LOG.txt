HOW TO IMPROVE MODEL ACCURACY & CHAT PERFORMANCE
================================================================================

A. OPENAI MODEL SETTINGS (Render -> skincare-api -> Environment)
----------------------------------------------------------------
Highest impact changes:

1) OPENAI_CHAT_MODEL=gpt-4o
   Best quality for product recommendations and skincare advice.
   Already set in render.yaml. Confirm it is active on Render.

2) OPENAI_INTENT_MODEL=gpt-4o-mini
   Keeps intent routing fast and cheap while chat uses gpt-4o.

3) OPENAI_EMBEDDING_MODEL=text-embedding-3-small
   Good balance of quality and cost for RAG search.

After changing env vars on skincare-api -> Save (auto redeploys).

B. UPLOAD KNOWLEDGE DOCUMENTS (HIGH IMPACT)
-------------------------------------------
Chat accuracy for policies and ingredients depends on your docs.
Go to /dashboard/knowledge and upload:
  - shipping-policy.txt
  - return-refund-policy.txt
  - ingredient-faq.txt
  - product usage guides per category

More detailed docs = better RAG answers with source grounding.

C. KEEP SHOPIFY CATALOG CURRENT
---------------------------------
Run Sync now after adding/editing products in Shopify.
Or schedule scripts/production_sync.ps1 every 6 hours.
Product embeddings re-index automatically after sync.

D. RAG TUNING (Render -> skincare-api Environment)
--------------------------------------------------
  RAG_TOP_K=5          Number of knowledge chunks retrieved (try 6-8)
  RAG_MIN_SCORE=0.12   Min similarity threshold (lower=more recall, higher=precision)
  PRODUCT_TOP_K=5      Products passed to AI (try 6-8)
  CHAT_HISTORY_LIMIT=12 Messages remembered (try 16 for longer consultations)

E. PROMPT TESTING CHECKLIST
---------------------------
Test these after each change:
  1. "I have oily, acne-prone skin. What moisturizer should I use?"
  2. "Can I use Niacinamide with Vitamin C?"
  3. "Build me a morning and night routine for sensitive skin."
  4. "Do you ship internationally?"
  5. "Compare your serums for dark spots."

Good answers should:
  - Only mention products from YOUR Shopify catalog
  - Explain why each product fits
  - Ask one follow-up when info is missing (not a long form)
  - Refuse medical diagnosis politely

F. PERFORMANCE (SPEED, NOT ACCURACY)
------------------------------------
Free tier Render:
  - First request after idle: 30-60 seconds (cold start)
  - Fix: upgrade skincare-api to Starter ($7/mo) for always-on

OpenAI latency:
  - gpt-4o is slower than gpt-4o-mini but more accurate
  - Use gpt-4o-mini for OPENAI_CHAT_MODEL only if speed > quality

Database:
  - Free Postgres on Render may add latency under load
  - Upgrade DB before 30-day expiry

G. WHAT NOT TO DO
-----------------
  - Do not set OPENAI_API_KEY in frontend (backend only)
  - Do not use localhost URLs in production env vars
  - Do not skip frontend redeploy after changing NEXT_PUBLIC_*
  - Do not expect order lookup until orders sync (PCD approval)
