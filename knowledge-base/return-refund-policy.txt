Glow Beauty Co. — Return and Refund Policy

Return Window
- Unopened and unused products may be returned within 30 days of delivery.
- Opened skincare products may be returned only if defective, damaged on arrival, or incorrect item shipped.

Non-Returnable Items
- Gift cards
- Final sale items (if marked at purchase)
- Opened products without defect (for hygiene reasons)

How to Start a Return
1. Contact support or use the chat assistant with your order number.
2. Explain the reason for return (wrong item, damage, allergic reaction to patch test, etc.).
3. We provide return instructions if the item is eligible.

Refund Timeline
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the return.
- Refunds go back to the original payment method.
- Bank processing times may add 3-5 additional business days.

Exchanges
- Exchanges are available for unopened products within 30 days, subject to stock availability.

Damaged on Arrival
- Report within 7 days with photos of the product and packaging.
- We typically send a replacement at no extra cost.

Allergic Reactions
- Stop using the product immediately.
- We cannot provide medical advice. Consult a dermatologist for persistent reactions.
- Contact us for return guidance if the product was recently purchased and unopened or minimally used per policy.
