Glow Beauty Co. — Shipping Policy

Domestic Shipping (United States)
- Orders are processed within 1-2 business days.
- Standard shipping arrives in 3-5 business days.
- Expedited shipping arrives in 2-3 business days where available.
- Orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping in the US.

International Shipping
- We ship to most international destinations.
- International delivery typically takes 7-14 business days.
- Customers are responsible for customs duties, import taxes, and local fees.
- Tracking is provided when the carrier supports it in the destination country.

Order Tracking
- Once your order ships, you receive a confirmation email with tracking information.
- You can ask our AI assistant for order status if you have your order number.

Shipping Restrictions
- We cannot ship to PO boxes for certain oversized items.
- Hazardous or restricted materials follow carrier and local regulations.

Damaged or Lost Packages
- If your package arrives damaged, contact us within 7 days with your order number and photos.
- We will replace or refund eligible damaged items.

Contact
- For shipping questions, use the chat assistant or contact support with your order number.
