Glow Beauty Co. — Hair Care Guide (Bangladesh Climate & Trends)

Purpose
Practical hair care guidance for humid Bangladesh weather, common South Asian hair concerns, and products we carry (shampoo, conditioner, oils, serums, masks, scalp treatments).

Climate Note (Bangladesh)
High humidity increases frizz, scalp sweat, and product buildup. Lightweight leave-ins, anti-frizz serums, and weekly clarifying (if oily scalp) help. Covered/hijab-friendly hair benefits from gentle, non-stripping shampoos and scalp refresh between washes.

Hair Fall / Thinning
Common causes: breakage from heat/color, tight styles, stress, nutrition, postpartum (general education only — not medical diagnosis).
Routine basics:
1. Gentle strengthening shampoo (biotin, keratin, protein blends)
2. Conditioner on mid-lengths to ends only
3. Weekly scalp massage with light oil (coconut, rosemary, or castor — patch test first)
4. Reduce heat; use heat protectant when blow-drying
Actives to look for: biotin, caffeine, keratin, rosemary, peptides (scalp serums).
Avoid: harsh sulfates daily on fragile hair, brushing wet hair aggressively, skipping conditioner.
When to escalate: sudden patchy loss, scalp pain, or no improvement after 3 months — see a dermatologist or trichologist.

Dandruff & Itchy Scalp
Mild flaking: anti-dandruff shampoo with pyrithione zinc or ketoconazole (use 2-3x weekly, leave on scalp 2-3 minutes).
Oily scalp + flakes: tea tree, salicylic scalp treatments, avoid heavy oils on scalp.
Dry scalp flakes: gentler shampoo, avoid over-washing; hydrate ends not scalp roots.
Do not scratch — worsens inflammation.

Dry & Damaged Hair
Signs: rough texture, split ends, breakage, dullness.
Repair approach: sulfate-free or gentle shampoo, rich conditioner, weekly hair mask with keratin/ceramides/protein-moisture balance.
Oils: coconut and almond oils popular in Bangladesh for pre-wash massage; rinse well to avoid buildup.
Bond repair masks (weekly) help color-treated or heat-damaged hair.

Frizz & Humidity
Use: smoothing shampoo/conditioner with keratin or silicone serums, leave-in creams for curls, anti-frizz serum on damp hair.
Technique: microfiber towel or cotton T-shirt dry; wide-tooth comb on damp hair; air-dry or diffuser when possible.
Bangladesh tip: light serums beat heavy creams in daily humidity unless hair is very dry.

Curly & Wavy Hair
Cleanse: sulfate-free or low-sulfate shampoo; co-wash or conditioner-only between full washes if needed.
Style: leave-in milk/cream, scrunch, avoid brushing dry curls.
Deep condition weekly. Protect hair at night (satin scarf or pillowcase optional).

Oily Scalp / Buildup
Clarify 1x weekly if using lots of styling products or living in humid cities.
Use lightweight shampoo at roots; conditioner on ends only.
Dry shampoo between washes for refresh — not a replacement for cleansing.

Sample Routines
Hair fall routine: strengthening shampoo → conditioner → rosemary/caffeine scalp serum (leave-on) → weekly oil massage.
Dandruff routine: anti-dandruff shampoo 3x/week → light conditioner on ends → tea tree scalp treatment weekly.
Curly hydration: curl shampoo → deep conditioner mask weekly → leave-in cream → hair oil on ends only.

Product Recommendation Voice
Say "we carry" or "from our store" — not "your catalog."
Ask hair type (straight, wavy, curly, coily), scalp type (oily, dry, balanced), and main concern before suggesting a full routine.
